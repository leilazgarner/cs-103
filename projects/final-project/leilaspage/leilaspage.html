If I Had Money: Tortilla Soup

Tortilla soup is a flavorful and comforting dish that would be perfect for a picnic if I had a bit more to spend. Here's a recipe:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add diced onions, garlic, and bell peppers. Saut√© until softened.
  3. Stir in diced tomatoes, chicken broth, shredded cooked chicken, and spices (such as cumin, chili powder, and salt).
  4. Simmer for 20-30 minutes.
  5. Serve hot, topped with crushed tortilla chips, shredded cheese, avocado slices, and a squeeze of lime.
